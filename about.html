ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Climate-related events are becoming more common and severe and governments are actively developing strategies to address the political, economic and national security implications.

A key global climate change initiative was the 2015 Paris Accords, the first time that most of the world’s leading countries came together to address the topic.

The key driver of global climate change is CO2 emissions. We’ve created visualizations to help you examine CO2 data from the years 2000 to 2014 - a period when emissions increased significantly - to have a better understanding of what world leaders knew as they participated in the Paris Accords.
